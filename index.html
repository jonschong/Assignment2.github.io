Raffles Place

High-end fitness experience in the heart of the financial district. Discover a spacious fitness playground spanning across more than 35,000 sq ft of space, conveniently located on Level 6 of One Raffles Place, just one minute away from the Raffles Place MRT station. The closest parking can be found at the basement of One Raffles Place.
